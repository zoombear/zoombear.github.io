Ynes Travel Timeline Map

Ynes Mexia was a botanist who collected plant samples throughout Mexico, Argentina, Chile, Brazil, Ecuador and Peru. Here I am in the process of mapping her travels, as well as creating a database of her plant sample data as she wrote in her travel journals.


Components of Upcoming Mapping Project:

- Interactive Timeline Map using D3.js
- MySQL database of Ynes' botanical records

Project Advisor - Kathryn Davis

This Mapping Project stems from Kathryn's qualitative research on Ynes Mexia's contribution to the field of Botany. Acting as mentor and thesis advisor to Vanessa Archambault and other SJSU students, Kathryn continues to successfully engage many budding geographers in the ongoing Ynes Mexia Anthology Project.

Documenting Ynes' travels

Ynes Mexia meticulously collected over __ plant samples in her 13 years of field study. Dr. Kathryn Davis is in the process of compiling an anthology of her travels and work.



Research Institutions

The California Academy of Sciences in San Francisco, CA and the Jepson Herbarium in Berkeley, CA hold many of Ynes' materials within their Archives. Additional research is currently being undertaken by geographers through both institutions.
